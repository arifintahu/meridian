## 2. Diagnosis

Identical shape to `exp-feb6609d` and `exp-397e2488`: every pool with
negative aggregate PnL (Flea-SOL, CATWIF-SOL, TrumpCoin-SOL, PUMPCAT-SOL,
BULLCAT-SOL, Wukong-SOL) is exactly the set that had a stop-loss event;
everything else is breakeven-to-positive. No entry-time signal reliably
predicts the stop-loss cohort — fee/TVL and entry_mcap are even reversed
from the prior round's readings, reinforcing "tail risk, not a selection
problem." The other structural finding stands too: `pumped_far_above_range`
+ `out_of_range` are 62% of all closes (64 of 104) at only +0.17–0.53% avg
PnL — capital returned the instant a position works, before it can compound
further. This is exactly the ceiling the same-night in-place-rebalancing
feature targets.
