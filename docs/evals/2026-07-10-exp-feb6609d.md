## 2. Diagnosis

The strategy is net-positive on everything except the tail: the 10 stop-losses (−$57.72) convert a +$35.21 book into −$22.51. Those stops gap *through* the −10% threshold to an avg realized −12.2% (worst −16.2%), all at 100% range efficiency — single-sided `bid_ask` positions bleeding straight down faster than the 10-min management cycle catches them, and faster than the `inRangeDrawdownPct −8/15min` sustain clock. Selection signals (organic, holders, mcap, TVL) are flat between winners and losers; the only differentiators are volatility and entry fee/TVL, both elevated in the stop-loss cohort — but the fat take-profit winners (ANSEMWIFE 5.64 vol, FOMO 4.15, Balloon 4.32) live in the same high-vol bucket, so a volatility cap is confounded. This is a **tail-risk problem, not a selection problem** — the direct lever is the stop level.
