## 2. Diagnosis

Fee-positive strategy sunk by tail risk: 18 stop-losses (−$109) more than erase
$95.82 of fees, and all profit sits in 16 long-held take-profit/trailing winners
(+$40) while 189 other positions net −$91. Volatility is the first screening signal
to separate across three runs (winners 4.43 vs losers 6.13); `vol ≥ 6` is a −$50/-$11-fee
money pit while `vol < 6` is break-even. It only surfaced now because the prior 91-close
run had too few high-vol stops (2) to show it — the "+$10.75 validated" conclusion was a
small-sample artifact. Two loss shapes hide in the 18 stops: 8 *fast* high-vol blowups
(vol ≥ 6, −$56, held 3–47m) that a volatility cap removes, and 10 *slow* directional
bleeds (vol < 6, −$53, held up to 474m — world, NEIL, WIF2) that no screening signal
predicts and the −10 stop already backstops. organic / mcap / fee-TVL remain dead or
dirty, consistent with both prior evals — no other threshold change is warranted.
