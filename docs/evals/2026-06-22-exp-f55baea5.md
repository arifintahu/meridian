## 2. Diagnosis

The entire negative result is **one position**. The win rate (77%), fee engine (+$10.86), and the profitable tail (PUMPED_ABOVE + TAKE_PROFIT = +$8.0) all work; ~49% of closes are near-zero OOR churn, which is expected for single-sided bid_ask. Kirby-SOL is a **management failure, not a selection failure**: it passed every screen, then bled while **97% in-range** (72 of 74 min) straight to −20.88% and hit the hard stop.

The newly enabled `IN_RANGE_DRAWDOWN` rule (−10%, 60-min wait) — built to catch exactly this in-range bleed before the hard stop — **fired 0 times in 53 closes**. The 60-min confirmation window is too slow: the dump reached the −20% stop before the soft-stop clock matured.

No **screening** change is defensible — every directional-looking signal (holder_count, entry_mcap) is contaminated by this single outlier, and `organic`/`fee_tvl`/`volatility` show zero separation. The one real lever is **tail risk on the management side**: across all 53 closes the −3%→−21% PnL band is empty except Kirby (nothing dipped past −3% and recovered), so tightening the downside cap has no observed false-stop cost in this run.
